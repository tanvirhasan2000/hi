ABOUT US

Md Emon Pt is from the Barisal division, Bangladesh. He was born on 30 October 2000. He started filmmaking in July 2018 with his own production house Ok Cut. He is an independent filmmaker. Filmmaking is his passion. He has already made some short films named "Friday" "Load Shedding" "Ratul's Story" and earlier a film named "Load Shedding" got an award and selection in several festivals. He also does Cinematography, Video Editing, Photography, and Graphic designing. He loves to tell stories with his own style by 24 frames of his society.
